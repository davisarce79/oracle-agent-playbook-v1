## The Six-Agent Pipeline
1. **Scout**: Finds local businesses on Google Maps (missing/weak sites).
2. **Intel**: Audits web presence (Mobile score, SEO, PageSpeed).
3. **Builder**: Generates custom sites via Claude 4.5 and Walkthrough videos.
4. **Outreach**: Sends personalized cold emails with live demo links.
5. **Closer**: Handles replies and objections via AI.
6. **Growth**: Monitors site health and handles long-term client care.

## Implementation Requirements
- **APIs**: Google Maps (Places), Anthropic (Claude 4.5), Vercel, SMTP (Zapmail), Suppabase/Local Video Service.
- **Workflow**: Automated lead sourcing -> Filter Grade A/B -> Deploy to Vercel -> Outreach.
