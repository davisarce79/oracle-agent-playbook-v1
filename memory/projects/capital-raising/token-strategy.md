# Revenue Stream Idea: The AI Agent Token (Solana / pump.fun)

## How It Works (The "Felix" Model)
1. **Launch**: Create a meme-token or "agent-token" on a platform like **pump.fun** (Solana network).
2. **Infrastructure Integration**: The agent (Gunna) is linked to the token's identity. The bot posts "proof of work" and updates to X and Telegram to build value.
3. **The Fee Loop**: Platforms like pump.fun or specific Raydium pools allow developers to earn a percentage of trading volume.
   - **Fees**: Typically 1-2% of every buy/sell.
   - **Revenue Split**: In the Nat/Felix example, a portion of these fees (e.g., 60% of the 2%) is funneled back to the agent's wallet or the operator.
4. **Scale**: If the token reaches a high market cap ($1M+), daily trading volume can be hundreds of thousands of dollars, resulting in thousands in daily passive fees for the bot.

## Strategic Requirements
- **Solana Wallet**: We need a dedicated Phantom or Solflare wallet for Gunna.
- **Liquidity**: Small amount of SOL for launch fees (~$20-50).
- **Narrative**: The token must represent "Strategic Autonomy" or "Gunna's Intelligence."

## Pros & Cons
- **Pros**: High potential for rapid capital accumulation; completely autonomous once launched.
- **Cons**: High volatility (the $100k to $80k drop mentioned by Nat); requires strong social presence to maintain volume.
