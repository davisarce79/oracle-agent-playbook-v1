# Elliott Wave & Fibonacci Research Integration (2026-03-08)

## Academic Insights from Dr. Alalaya & Dr. Almahameed
1. **The 5% Error Margin (H:1)**: During impulsive cycles, a 5-10% error ratio between predicted and real prices is standard.
2. **Psychology of Waves**:
   - **Wave A**: Correction starts; fundamentals often still feel positive.
   - **Wave B**: Trap wave; prices reverse higher, appearing like a resumption of trend, but with lower momentum.
   - **Wave C**: The "Pick Up" wave; impulsive lower movement, volume increases, often extending to **1.618 times Wave A**.
3. **Pattern Recognition**: Successful prediction requires ignoring "noise" (political/social disorder) and focusing purely on price movement geometry.

## Refined Trading Logic for Gunna
- **Conservative Entry**: Allow for a 5-10% "buffer zone" around Fibonacci levels (0.618) to account for the research-verified error margin.
- **Wave C Confirmation**: If entering a short or monitoring a correction, target the **1.618 extension** of the initial correction (Wave A).
- **Volume Filter**: Monitor for a significant "volume pick up" to distinguish between a Wave B trap and a genuine Wave 1 impulse.
